Hospital Workflow Demo Guide

Follow these steps to experience the complete Nightingale platform workflow.

1. Sign In: Log in as Registration Staff on the Login page.
2. Patient ID Extraction: Go to Thai ID Scan and upload a mock ID or use a camera to extract identity via OCR to auto-fill registration.
3. Verify Data: Admins or Registration staff review extracted data in the Verification Queue to securely match or create a profile.
4. AI Summarization: Log in as a Doctor or Nurse, access Department Summary or ICU Analysis. Click Generate Summary for instant, bilingual clinical insights.
5. Evidence Traceback: Inside AI summaries, click data points to reveal the transparent evidence traceback from original CSV/JSON sources.
6. Longitudinal Timeline: Open the Medical Timeline to trace long-term chronic conditions seamlessly across visits.
7. System Audit: Log in as Admin to review the Audit Log trails.
